Target Audience

The audience is people 18-year-old ahead that is interested in business or is the owner of one business and is looking for a network to make it grow. Is interested in opportunities to get investors or invest, to get help with the legal environment, to get information about markets or tendencies, etc. To know everything related to owning a business and making it bigger and better.
